Beef Quesadilla

Ingredients

  • 1lb of Lean Ground Beef
  • 1 Onions
  • Seasonings-Salt,Pepper, Paprika, Cajun
  • 250g of mix of Grated Cheddar and Mozzarella Cheese
  • Small Can of beans of your choice
  • 1Tbps of Tomato Paste

    • Instructions

      Cook Onions on medium-high heat for 2-4 minutes
      Once water starts to form at the bottom of dish, remove water
      Add Ground beef to pan until fully browned
      Add Tomato Paste along with cooked onions and stir for 3 minutes
      Add beans and stir
      Apply Olive Oil to Tortilla on each side
      Place cooked beef mix to half of tortilla with generous handful of cheese mix
      Firmly fold and place to pan to heat for 1-2 min
      Flip over one time for 30second and remove from pan
      Bon Appetit
