Southern Breakfast Potatoets

Ingredients

  • 4 Potatoes
  • 2 Onions
  • Seasonings-Salt,Pepper, Paprika
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper

    • Instructions

      Cut Potatoes to extremely thin slices
      Cut Onions in heavy slices
      Cut Peppers to small little cubes
      Heat pan to medium-high heat and then add Olive Oil
      Add Onions and cook for 4-5 minutes
      Add Potatoes with seasonings and cook for 3-7 minutes
      Add Pepper and stir vigriously for 4-6 minutes
      Place lid and let cook for 3-4 minutes
      Once potatoes are extremely tender turn off heat
      Bon Appetit
