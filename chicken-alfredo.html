Cajun Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients

  • 0.5 Onion
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic
  • 2tbsp Butter
  • 2lb of Chicken Thighs
  • 1lb of Mushrooms
  • 450ml Whipping Cream
  • 100g Reggy Parmaesan
  • Salt, Pepper, Paprika

    • Instructions

      Cut Onion, Garlic, Mushroom, Chicken Thighs
      On Medium Heat add some Olive Oil
      Add Chicken Thigh and add seasoning
      Remove Thighs and add butter in the pot
      Saute Onions for 2-3 minutes
      Add Mushrooms-cook for 4-5 minutes
      Add Garlic and Cook for 30 seconds
      Add Whipping Cream and let it boil for 4 minutes
      Add Parmaesan and boil for 2 minutes
      Re-Add Chicken and cooked pasta and stir
